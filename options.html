Welcome to the DocGraph Spade Extension!

Mine your browser for clinical data. For more information, please see: http://spade.docgraph.org.

Clinical Data Spade Settings

This setup enables this extension to allow medical students to donate portions of their browser history to an online database of browsing patterns that will become an open data set.

  4. To enable, disable, update, or remove this extension:

    1. Click the Chrome menu on the browser toolbar.
    2. Select Tools.
    3. Select Extensions.
    4. Click the enabled checkbox for this extension to enable or disable it.
    5. Click the trashcan icon for this extension to remove it.
    6. To show this page, click the Options link for this extension.
